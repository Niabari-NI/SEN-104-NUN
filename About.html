About Us

The reason behind this

This website was created due to the fact that my fellow course mates

and I had some troube with understanding the notes at times. Others

could not even access the notes. Then there was also the problem of

not being able to get the answer to some assignments that we struggled

with. Due to such problems, we would spend countless hours scrolling through

the internet and in the end not get any solution to the assignments or

just end up submitting absolute rubbish.

This then got me thinking, "Isn't there a way to make things just even

a little easier for us?". Then the idea of this website came up.

To do this even turned out to be easier than expected, thanks to my

ability to grasp code easily and the leassons that I was able to

understand at the University.

Now all I hope is that this website proves to be of as much help as

I would love it to be for those entering and are still in the university.

The website usage is absoluty free so please enjoy using this wonderful site.
